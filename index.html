Refined in the dorm rooms of Isaiah Polite

Ingredients!

  • 1 Pack of Shin Ramen Black
  • 4 Slices of Smoked Salmon (or other protein)
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Slice of Cheese (optional)
  • 1 Stalk of Green Onion
  • 4 pieces of seaweed (optional)

Step by Step Guide!

  1. Put two eggs in boiling water for 6 mintues and 30 seconds
  2. Place eggs in ice water for 3 minutes; cut up one shoot of green onion
  3. Place flavor packet, vegetables, and chopped green onion inside of the boiling water; add noodles.
  4. Place one slice of cheese on top of noodles half way through cooking process. After the cheeese disolves stir the noodles.
  5. Tap the top of the egg with a spoon and begin to peel carefully! If successful the egg should be soft boiled.
  6. Pour noodles from pot into eating container. Place Salmon (or other desired protein on the side of the bowl), seaweed, and eggs. Serve and enjoy! üòã
